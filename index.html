Why Maskmaxxing?

Urban pollution is invisible yet dangerous. Millions of people commute every day without realizing the air quality they breathe. Conventional masks only filter, but fail to communicate when they are saturated or ineffective.

Maskmaxxing introduces a **visual pollution-reactive layer** that changes its pattern and color as pollutants accumulate. This makes pollution visible, encourages timely mask replacement, and raises environmental awareness.

4-Layer Smart Filtration System

A carefully engineered multi-layer system that balances filtration efficiency, breathability, comfort, and visual feedback.

Spunbond Polypropylene

Outer protective layer that blocks large particles, repels droplets, and provides mechanical strength.

Melt-blown Filter

High-efficiency filtration core capturing PM2.5, bacteria, and microscopic pollutants.

Smart Nanofiber Layer

Reactive membrane that changes color and pattern based on pollution exposure level.

PET Comfort Support

Lightweight and breathable support layer ensuring long-lasting comfort.
